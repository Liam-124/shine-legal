Privacy Policy for Shine Social

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Shine Social, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, username, email, age, location (town/state), gender, relationship status
  • Content: Posts, photos, videos, messages you create
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (likes, shares, friend connections)
  • Device Information: Basic device type and app version for technical support

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Shine Social service
  • Connect you with friends and show relevant local content
  • Ensure safety and prevent abuse
  • Send important updates about your account
  • Respond to support requests

3. Information Sharing

Your information is shared:

  • With Friends: Your profile and posts marked for friends are visible to your accepted friends
  • Locally: Posts marked as "Local" are visible to other users in your town
  • Anonymous Posts: When posting anonymously, your identity is hidden from other users

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Track you across other websites or apps

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: Industry-standard PostgreSQL database with encryption
  • Cloud Storage: Images and videos stored with secure access controls
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View your profile information at any time
  • Update: Edit your profile and posts
  • Delete Content: Remove individual posts
  • Block Users: Prevent specific users from seeing your content or contacting you
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data (Settings â†’ Delete Account)

6. Children's Privacy

Shine Social is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover a user is under 13, we will delete their account immediately.

7. Data Retention

  • Posts are kept for 24 hours in feeds, but stored permanently until you delete them
  • Messages are stored until you delete your account
  • When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days

8. Third-Party Services

We use these trusted services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • We do not use analytics or advertising services that track you

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Shine Social after changes means you accept the updated policy.
